National Grid - Spring Insight Week

During this experience, I collaborated with a team of six students to develop a comprehensive business plan aimed at helping the National Grid transition toward net zero emissions — all within a challenging time limit of 4 hours.

As the primary group communicator, I took on a leadership role by managing all inquiries, facilitating clear and accurate communication, and ensuring the team remained aligned with our project goals. This enabled us to achieve 100% alignment and maintain focus under pressure.

We presented our pitch to a panel of judges and an audience of over 20 participating teams, showcasing our plan’s innovation and feasibility. Our team earned an impressive 4th place overall.
